Collapse the Possibilities!

Decide what you are going to do at the click of a button.

Ask a yes or no question (as specific as you can) and press the "Answer Me!" button.

(e.g. should I study for the biology exam that is 4 months away or the math exam that is 2 months away?)

What does this website do?

This website exists to help people that have a hard time making choices.

I used to have a lot of anxiety about making important decisions, and used random number generators to help me decide. So I made this website that will give you a "yes" or "no" answer to help you feel more decisive or confident about your decisions!
